Guess The Number!

Guess the Number is a game of chance that involves absolutely no skill. In this game the computer will roll a 6 sided die. Your job is to predict whether an EVEN or an ODD number will be rolled. Each guess costs $10, and you start the game with $50. If you guess incorrectly, you will lose your $10 bet, but if you guess correctly you will win $10. The game ends when your run out of money, or when you choose to leave this webpage!
