BTS, one of the most representative boy bands in Korea, and its label Big Hit Entertainment are staging the LOVE MYSELF campaign, jointly with UNICEF. BTS and its label entered into a partnership officially with the Korean Committee for UNICEF affiliated with the United Nations on November 1, 2017, in an attempt to sponsor a campaign called #ENDviolence—UNICEF’s global campaign aimed at ensuring children and teens in the world lead safe and healthy lives without the fear of violence. In this regard, BTS has created its own campaign called LOVE MYSELF, pursuing love and a better place to live for all. This is not a one-off event. For the next two years at least, BTS will continue to follow through with this campaign, along with #ENDviolence, and work to raise a BTS LOVE MYSELF fund.

The United Nations Children's Fund is a United Nations program headquartered in New York City that provides humanitarian and developmental assistance to children and mothers in developing countries.
